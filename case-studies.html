Case Studies

Government Ransomware Defense

When a local municipal office was hit with a ransomware attack, Cicada 3301 was called in. Within hours, our team deployed forensic tools to isolate the threat, trace its entry point, and prevent further encryption of critical data.

Our specialists recovered 98% of encrypted files and implemented a real-time monitoring system to prevent future attacks.

Banking DDoS Attack Response

A Philippine-based fintech platform suffered a Distributed Denial-of-Service (DDoS) attack causing massive downtime. We immediately launched our rapid response playbook using traffic filtering, IP geofencing, and cloud mitigation.

The service was restored within 3 seconds, with zero data loss. Our efforts were later published in a local cybersecurity report.

E-Commerce Breach Recovery

After a breach that leaked over 5,000 customer records, an SME retailer hired us to investigate. We found misconfigured APIs and outdated encryption protocols.

Cicada 3301 implemented end-to-end encryption, enabled MFA for admin logins, and trained their staff on phishing prevention. Customer trust was restored.
